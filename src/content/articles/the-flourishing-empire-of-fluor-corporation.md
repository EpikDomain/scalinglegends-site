Fluor Corporation's journey began with its founder, John Simon Fluor Sr., a Swiss immigrant who arrived in America in 1888 with only $100. From those humble beginnings, Fluor grew into one of the world's largest engineering and construction companies.

This episode traces the remarkable arc from a one-man operation to a Fortune 500 global powerhouse, and the lessons every contractor can apply to their own scaling journey.

## What You'll Learn

- How Fluor scaled from a single founder to a global empire
- The role of engineering excellence in commanding premium contracts
- Diversification strategies that protect against market cycles
- Why long-term vision beats short-term profits in construction scaling

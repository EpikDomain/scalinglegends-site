The global data center industry is entering a period of unprecedented growth and investment, primarily catalyzed by the intensive computing requirements of artificial intelligence. For construction companies, this represents one of the biggest opportunities in a generation.

This episode breaks down the data center construction boom -- the scale of investment, the unique building challenges, and how contractors can position themselves to capture this work.

## What You'll Learn

- The scale of data center construction spending ($25B+ monthly in new starts)
- Power and water infrastructure challenges driving specialized construction demand
- Why data center builds require different skills than traditional commercial construction
- How to position your firm for data center contracts in 2026 and beyond

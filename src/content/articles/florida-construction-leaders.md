Lemartec, now a MasTec Company, represents the rare breed of construction firm that mastered both horizontal infrastructure and vertical construction. Based in Florida, they've built a reputation for tackling complex projects in one of America's fastest-growing states.

This episode explores how Florida's construction leaders navigate hurricane codes, rapid population growth, and the unique demands of building in the Sunshine State.

## What You'll Learn

- How acquisition by a larger firm (MasTec) can accelerate growth
- Navigating Florida's unique building codes and hurricane requirements
- The vertical vs. horizontal construction debate and why the best firms do both
- Scaling strategies specific to high-growth Sun Belt markets

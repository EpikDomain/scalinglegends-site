Is your construction business ready for the 2026 pivot? Arizona's market is shifting from speculative new builds to high-ROI green renovations and specialized industrial infrastructure.

Phoenix builders who recognized this shift early are winning big. This episode breaks down the expectation gap between what most contractors think the market wants and what it actually demands.

## What You'll Learn

- Why the Phoenix construction market is pivoting from new builds to renovations
- How green building certifications are becoming profit centers, not cost centers
- The industrial infrastructure boom driven by semiconductor and data center demand
- Positioning your company for the next wave of Arizona construction spending

Webcor has spent over 50 years mastering the art of construction in one of America's most challenging urban environments -- San Francisco.

This episode dives into what it takes to specialize in a single market and dominate it. From navigating seismic requirements to building in dense urban cores, Webcor's story is a masterclass in niche expertise.

## What You'll Learn

- Why geographic specialization can be more profitable than spreading thin
- How to navigate complex regulatory environments as a competitive advantage
- Building relationships with city planners and inspectors as a growth strategy
- The economics of urban construction and why specialists command premium margins

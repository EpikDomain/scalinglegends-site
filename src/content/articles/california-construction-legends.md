DPR Construction didn't wait for the industry to embrace green building -- they pioneered it. As an employee-owned firm, DPR has built a culture of innovation that has made them one of California's most respected construction companies.

In this episode, we explore how California's construction legends built their empires, from DPR's employee ownership model to the strategies that set West Coast builders apart from the rest of the nation.

## What You'll Learn

- How employee ownership drives performance and retention in construction
- The green building revolution and why California led the way
- Strategies for scaling in one of America's most regulated construction markets
- Why culture matters more than equipment when building a construction empire
